Lawn Mowing & Garden Services in Te Aroha

Te Aroha is where Four Seasons Lawn Services is based, so it's where our rounds run most often — from in-town sections at the base of Mount Te Aroha to rural properties out toward the Waihou River.

Commercial & residential work in Te Aroha

We run regular mowing, hedging and garden maintenance rounds for both homes and businesses in Te Aroha, plus one-off tidy-ups for sections that need bringing back under control. Being based locally means shorter travel time between jobs, which keeps scheduling flexible if your grass has grown faster than expected after a wet spell.

Services available in Te Aroha
