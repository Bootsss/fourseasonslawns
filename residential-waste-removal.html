Residential Green Waste Removal

Grass clippings, hedge trimmings and garden waste all need to go somewhere after a tidy-up. We clear it away as part of every mowing and garden job, and also take on standalone waste removal for bigger clean-ups.

What's included

  • Green waste cleared after every mowing, hedging or garden visit
  • Standalone removal for larger garden clean-ups
  • No waste left piled up in the garden or on the kerb

Why homeowners choose us

  • Included as standard with our regular services
  • Saves a trip to the tip after a big garden tidy-up
  • Straightforward pricing for one-off removal jobs

Frequently asked questions

Is waste removal included with mowing?

Yes, clippings and trimmings are cleared from paths and driveways as part of every visit.

Can you take away waste from a job I've already done myself?

In most cases, yes — get in touch with roughly how much there is and we'll confirm.
