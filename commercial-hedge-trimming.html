Commercial Hedge & Boundary Trimming

A tidy boundary hedge or screening line says a lot about a business before anyone walks through the door. We trim and shape commercial hedges on a set schedule so frontages, car park screening and boundary lines stay sharp all year.

What's included

  • Shaping and height reduction for boundary and screening hedges
  • Straight, level lines along car parks, driveways and street frontages
  • Seasonal scheduling — a heavier cut in spring growth, tidy-ups through summer and autumn
  • Green waste removed from site after every visit

Why commercial clients choose us

  • Street-facing presentation kept consistent between visits
  • Scheduling built around your hedge's actual growth rate, not a fixed guess
  • Works alongside our mowing contracts, or as a standalone service

Frequently asked questions

How often do commercial hedges need trimming?

Most boundary and screening hedges need a proper trim two to three times a year — a heavier cut in late spring, then tidy-ups through summer and autumn. Fast-growing varieties may need more frequent attention.

Do you take the trimmings away?

Yes, green waste from hedge trimming is cleared from site as part of the job.

Can this be bundled with mowing?

Yes — most commercial clients have hedge trimming scheduled alongside their regular mowing round to keep the whole property tidy in one visit.
